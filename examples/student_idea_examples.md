# Student chatbot idea examples

## Customer support bot
- Company: online clothing store, gym, hotel, bank, phone provider, food delivery company
- Goal: answer common customer questions and escalate difficult cases
- Useful data: FAQ, return policy, product catalog, opening hours
- Useful tools: search_company_data, create_support_ticket, current_datetime

## Internal company assistant
- Company: consulting firm, school, HR department, software team
- Goal: help employees find internal processes
- Useful data: onboarding manual, HR policies, meeting notes, process documents
- Useful tools: search_company_data, current_datetime, calculator

## Technical troubleshooting bot
- Company: factory, solar panel installer, appliance repair company, IoT device company
- Goal: help technicians diagnose common problems
- Useful data: manuals, error code tables, maintenance procedures
- Useful tools: search_company_data, calculator, create_support_ticket

## Sales assistant
- Company: travel agency, SaaS company, course provider, real estate agency
- Goal: recommend services and explain offers
- Useful data: service list, price table, comparison table, terms and conditions
- Useful tools: search_company_data, calculator
