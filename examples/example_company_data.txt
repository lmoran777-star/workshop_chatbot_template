Example company: SolarFix Academy

SolarFix Academy trains technicians to install and maintain small solar panels for homes and schools.

Common customer problems:
1. The solar panel is producing less energy than expected.
Possible causes: dirt on the panel, shade from trees, loose cables, old battery, or wrong panel angle.
Recommended first steps: check if the panel is clean, remove shade if possible, verify cable connections, and compare today with other sunny days.

2. The battery does not charge.
Possible causes: damaged charge controller, disconnected battery, old battery, or very low sunlight.
Recommended first steps: check the battery cable, inspect the charge controller lights, and test again during strong sunlight.

Support rules:
- Never ask customers to open dangerous electrical components.
- If there is smoke, burning smell, or exposed wiring, tell the customer to disconnect the system if safe and contact a certified technician.
- For warranty claims, ask for product model, purchase date, and a short description of the issue.
